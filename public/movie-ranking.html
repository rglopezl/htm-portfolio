Best Movies According to Mighty Gabriel

My Top 4 Movies of ALL TIME!!

Jojo Rabbit


Best war parody movie ever. Great characters. Great way to laugh over the ridiculousness of WW2 and how stupid are wars in general.

Ratatouille


My most favorite animated movie from Disney since I watched it. The cuttest characters. Nice storyline with an animal with an extraordinary abilitie as protagonist. The music just magnificent. And on top of that the display of the beauty of gastronomy will always be appreciated by me.

Kimi no na wa


Sad. Lovely. Thrilling. Visualy astonishing. All that in combination with the best sound that they could have. Makes this travel in time, body changing story just amazing.

Tetris


The directos of this movie just did their best on transformign the story of the creation of one of the most impactful games of gaming industry in a story anyone can enjoy. Good plot twist. Good action escenes. Overall it's a great movie that more than just entertain us teaches us a story which who would've thought was this interesting.
